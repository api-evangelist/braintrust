Braintrust has released Topics as a generally available feature that automatically clusters trace summaries at scale. The company is shifting from passive AI observability to active observability, where systems continuously work in the background to identify patterns and surface insights without requiring explicit queries. This approach aims to cost-effectively apply intelligence to production data, helping developers distill information into actionable insights.
